The principal is the head of the school, responsible for overseeing all academic and administrative functions. They play a crucial role in creating a positive learning environment, guiding teachers, and supporting students. A principal also acts as a liaison between the school and the community, promoting collaboration and engagement. Their leadership inspires both students and staff to achieve excellence in education.
